# Safety Stock Recommendation Tool

Streamlit app that generates safety stock recommendations per material from SAP MARC and MB51 extracts.

## Setup

```bash
pip install -r requirements.txt
streamlit run app.py
```

## Usage

1. Upload the MARC extract (.xlsx) and MB51 extract (.xlsx).
2. Adjust parameters in the sidebar:
   - Service level (90 / 95 / 97.5 / 99%)
   - Demand signal (641, 601, or both)
   - Lead time source (TRLT or PDT+GRT)
   - Days of Supply benchmark
   - Delta alignment threshold
3. Review the KPI summary, filterable recommendation table, and per-material demand charts.
4. Download the formatted Excel output (includes a methodology sheet).

## Methodology

- Statistical SS = Z x sigma(monthly demand) x sqrt(lead time in months)
- DoS SS = (avg monthly demand / 30) x benchmark days
- Recommended SS = ceiling(max(Statistical SS, DoS SS))
- Zero-demand months are included in the standard deviation to correctly capture intermittent demand.
- Materials in MARC with no movement history are flagged, not dropped.

## Adapting to different SAP extracts

If your extract columns are named differently, edit the `MARC_COLS` and `MB51_COLS`
dictionaries at the top of `app.py`. Nothing else needs to change.

## Required columns

**MARC:** Material, Plnt, Typ, MRPCn, Safety Stock, Fix. lot size, PDT, GRT, TRLT, ABC

**MB51:** Material, Material Description, Plant, Movement Type, Posting Date, Qty in unit of entry, Unit of Entry
